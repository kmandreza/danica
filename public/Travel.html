TRAVEL RECOMMENDATIONS:

The closet airport to the Santa Cruz/Capitola area is the San Jose International Airport. Once you arrive, there are three general options for transportation to your hotel.

  1. Rent a car
  2. Take a shuttle service. We recommend the Santa Cruz Airport Flyer Shuttle Service. A one way trip from the San Jose airport will cost you around $40 per person
  3. Take a taxi

For any questions related to our hotel or travel suggestions, please feel free to email us at sanchiswedding@gmail.com.
